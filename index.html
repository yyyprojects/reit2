The QBI Tax Advantage

A powerful (but temporary) tax rule, the Qualified Business Income (QBI) deduction, can significantly lower your tax bill on REIT dividends. It allows you to deduct 20% of your qualified REIT dividends from your taxable income.

How It Works: An Example

If you receive a $1,000 dividend and are in the 24% tax bracket, you would normally pay $240 in taxes. With the QBI deduction:

  • 1. You deduct 20% of the dividend ($200).
  • 2. You are only taxed on the remaining $800.
  • 3. Your tax bill is just $192.

This simple deduction lowers your effective tax rate from 24% to just 19.2%.
